EVENT DESCRIPTION

THEME:

Quintessence of Life


Waves 2017, invite you to display your master photography skills in these following events:

Negate:

A laugh, a frown
A glare, a clown.
As humans we have an array of emotions we display. Some are similar while some are severely contrasting. . However, sometimes you have to dive deeper and read into the more subtle hints that indicate the mood of a person.

Monochrome:

The world is an outburst of colors. However, we gave away the twist in this tale in the name of the event. This competition allows you to capture whichever object your heart desires in either black and white or in varying tones of only one color.

Rules

- Photos must meet the following requirements: JPG or JPEG file format, 72dpi. Ideally, the photo dimensions should be 1024x768 pixels.
- Photographs should not have been previously published in any print and digital media.
- Minimal photo editing is permissible, but altering the photograph is not allowed. We assume that every photo submission is legitimate.
However, if a photo is suspected of alteration, it will be rejected. The final decision rests with the Waves Controls.
- By submitting your photographs you accept that you are the copyright owner of the photograph and that you agree to our terms and conditions.
- Every picture should have a suitable caption pertaining to the theme - No camera restriction.
- Theme for the event will be released through our social media platform.
- Photographs must be clicked in the year 2017.
- Photographs without EXIF data (and hence the date) will not be accepted. Make sure that your camera date is set before you click a picture.
- In photo editing only global editing i.e. cropping and adjustment of hue/saturation; brightness/contrast levels are allowed. Photo-morphing, manipulations or local editing is not allowed.
- Participants have to submit both the edited and original entries for judging.
- Participants have to submit an overall description of the photo-story not exceeding 25 words. Individual photo-captions are not allowed.
