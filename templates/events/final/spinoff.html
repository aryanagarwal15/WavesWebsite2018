Spin-off is a DJing Event which primarily focuses on bringing upcoming Djs
of the nation together to compete and showcase their skills on one of the
best platforms in the Country judged by the Gurus of the cultures
themselves. The festive Goan Atmosphere and an audience from all across
the nation is bound to create an intoxicating and ecstatic “never has been
before” experience that a DJ could ask for. The Winner of the event Gets a
once in a lifetime chance to perform on the Iconic EDM Night of Waves.

Rules
Eliminations :
- Each Contestant will be given 10 minutes to mix his/her tracks on stage. Pre Mixed tracks will not be considered and may result in disqualification of the contestant.
- The Participants are allowed to use their own Headphones if required and allowed by the Honourable Judges.
- Contestants may not use any external aid once his/her time begins on stage.
- Winners shall qualify to participate in the finals during Waves 2017 at Goa.
- Failure to abide by the Judges, Organising Committee and the rules set forth will result in immediate disqualification from the event.

Semi-Finals and Finals :
- Each Contestant will be given 10 minutes to mix his/her tracks on stage. Pre Mixed tracks will not be considered and may result in disqualification of the contestant.
- The Participants are allowed to use their own Headphones if required and allowed by the Honourable Judges.
- Contestants may not use any external aid once his/her time begins on stage.
- Winner of Spin-off will get to perform on the EDM night of Waves
- Failure to abide by the Judges, Waves Control and the rules set forth will result in immediate disqualification from the event.

udging Criteria:
Contestants will be judged on the following factors out of a score of 10 (5+5).
A score out of 5 based on the following (5):
- Mixing Techniques and Track Selection
- Response from the audience
and another out of 5 based on (5)
- Micing (Mic Use)
- Body Language
- Appearance
