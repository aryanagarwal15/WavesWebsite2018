Team Composition: 1-3 members
Teams per college: 4 (max)
Time Limit: Round 1: 60 minutes
Registration: online/ on-fest

Rules
Elimination Round: - Each team will get 60 minutes to design and paint their T- shirts.
- 8 teams will be shortlisted for the next round.
- Only fabric paint are allowed to be used on the T shirts.
- Stationery to be brought by the participants.
- T-shirts will be provided by us according to the sizes required.

Judging Criteria for Elimination Round:
- Aesthetic Appeal.
- Creativity.
- Other parameters and weightage to be decided by the judges and they will be communicated to the participants before the event commences.

Final Round:
- Teams have to model the T-shirt designed in the first round on one of their team members and present it to the judges.

Judging Criteria for Round2 :
1. Presentation of the model.
2. Incorporation of the theme.
3. Other parameters and weightage to be decided by the judges and they will be communicated to the participants before the event commences.
